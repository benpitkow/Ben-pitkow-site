Why I do this

There's a lot of creditworthy businesses not getting the loans they deserve.

Legacy underwriting models were built for a certain kind of borrower. A lot of solid small businesses just don't fit that mold, and they get turned away even when they'd pay back every dollar. That gap is real, it's large, and it's not going away on its own.

I've been inside these systems long enough to know exactly where the friction is. Better data, smarter decisioning engines, and digital-first delivery can close that gap while keeping credit quality intact. That's what I'm focused on building.

Better Decisioning
Alternative data and modern credit models that capture actual repayment capacity, not just whether someone fits a legacy template
Larger TAM
Reaching the creditworthy borrowers that traditional underwriting consistently misses, and growing the pie in the process
Scalable Ops
Digital-native workflows that bring down cost-to-originate without sacrificing the credit discipline that keeps portfolios healthy
